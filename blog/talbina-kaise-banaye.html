Talbina Kaise Banaye? Ghar Par Talbina Banane Ki Aasan Recipe

Talbina ek purani aur poshtik ghiza hai jo jau (barley) se banayi jati hai. Isse breakfast ya light meal ke roop me liya ja sakta hai. Aaiye jante hain ghar par Talbina banane ka aasan tarika.

Talbina Banane Ke Liye Samagri

  • 2 tablespoon barley (jau) ka powder
  • 1 cup doodh ya pani
  • 1 teaspoon shahad (optional)
  • Khajoor powder ya dry fruits (optional)

Talbina Banane Ka Tarika

  1. Ek pan me doodh ya pani garam karein.
  2. Usme dheere dheere barley powder milayein.
  3. Lagatar chalate rahein taki guthliyan na banein.
  4. 5-10 minute tak halki aanch par pakayein.
  5. Jab mixture creamy ho jaye to gas band kar dein.
  6. Swad ke liye shahad, khajoor powder ya dry fruits mila sakte hain.
  7. Garam garam serve karein.

Talbina ko breakfast me ya shaam ke healthy snack ke roop me liya ja sakta hai.
